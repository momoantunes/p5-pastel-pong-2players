# p5-pastel-pong-2players
a pong game for 2 players created using p5

## HOW TO PLAY  ##
-> Player ONE (left)
To move your bar UP: Press W
To move your bar DOWN: Press S

-> Player TWO (right)
To move your bar UP: Press UP ARROW
To move your bar DOWN: Press DOWN ARROW
